Everything you need for browser management

Powerful features designed for power users with multiple browsers and profiles

Profile-Aware Routing

Automatically detects all your browser profiles (personal, work, dev) and routes URLs to the right one.

URL Groups

Group related domains together. Built-in templates for Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace included.

Clipboard Monitoring

Optionally watch your clipboard for URLs and route them with a single click from the system tray.

Smart Notifications

Toast notifications let you quickly create rules for new domains when no match is found.

Multi-Profile Picker

When a rule has multiple profiles, a picker appears letting you choose where to open the link.

Backup & Restore

Export and import your configuration. Automatic rolling backups keep your data safe.
